When it comes to buying new construction homes, few realtors stand out like Diane Hibbs. Known for a client-first approach and consistent results, Diane Hibbs has earned a reputation as one of the area's most trusted professionals.

Experience with New Builds:
Diane has years of experience helping buyers through the new construction process. She understands the unique steps and timing that come with purchasing a home that's still being built.

Builder Relationships:
Diane has developed strong working relationships with many local and regional builders. This allows her to give her clients inside knowledge, helpful contacts, and sometimes even early access to new developments.

Guidance Through Every Phase:
From choosing a floor plan to reviewing contracts and attending walkthroughs, Diane is with her clients at every step. She helps buyers understand the process and make smart decisions for their future homes.

Protecting Her Clients’ Interests:
Builders have their own agents who represent their interests. Diane steps in to make sure buyers have someone who’s looking out for them—explaining terms, negotiating better options, and spotting red flags.

Knowledge of Upgrades and Incentives:
Diane knows which upgrades add value and which ones don’t. She shows clients how to maximize builder incentives and avoid spending money on features they won't really use.

Navigating Contracts:
New construction contracts can be long and confusing. Diane takes the time to explain the details so her clients know exactly what they’re signing.

Tracking Progress and Deadlines:
Homes under construction don’t always finish on time. Diane keeps in regular contact with the builder to make sure things are moving and to keep her clients informed of changes.

Handling Inspections and Walkthroughs:
Even brand-new homes can have issues. Diane attends inspections and walkthroughs to help her buyers spot problems, ask questions, and get anything fixed before move-in.

Problem Solver:
If surprises come up during the build, Diane doesn’t panic—she finds solutions. Her calm, focused approach helps her clients stay confident throughout the process.

Local Insight:
Diane knows which new neighborhoods are growing, which schools are best, and what resale value might look like down the road. She uses this knowledge to help her clients choose a home that fits their life and their future.
